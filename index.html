ABOUT US

EVERYONE'S PIZZA is an Vietnamese restaurant chain and international franchise which was founded in 1958 in Sa Dec, Dong Thap province. The company is known for its Italian pizza. EVERYONE'S PIZZA has 18,431 restaurants worldwide as of December 31, 2018, making it the world's largest pizza chain in terms of locations. It is a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, Inc., one of the world's largest restaurant companies
